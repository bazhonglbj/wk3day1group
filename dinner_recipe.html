Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

A classic Italian dish that's perfect for a hearty evening meal. Rich meat sauce combined with spaghetti makes this dish a favorite for many.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g ground beef or pork
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the ground meat to the skillet and cook until browned. Break the meat up with a spatula as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the sauce simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the sauce has thickened, toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix well to coat the pasta with the sauce.
  6. Serve the spaghetti bolognese in bowls, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

All done! Enjoy your Spaghetti Bolognese!
