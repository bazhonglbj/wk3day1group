Grilled Chicken Sandwich Recipe

This grilled chicken sandwich is both healthy and delicious with grilled chicken, fresh lettuce, and juicy tomatoes. Perfect with some mayonnaise or honey mustard.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • Mayonnaise or honey mustard, optional

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place chicken on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked through.
  4. Remove chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  5. Place buns on the grill, cut side down, and toast for 1-2 minutes.
  6. To assemble the sandwich, place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each bun. Add a slice of tomato, followed by a grilled chicken breast. If desired, spread some mayonnaise or honey mustard on the top half of the bun.
  7. Place the top bun on the chicken and serve immediately.

Enjoy your Grilled Chicken Sandwich!
